History

Main articles: History of blogging and online diary

The term "weblog" was coined by Jorn Barger on December 17, 1997. The short form "blog" was later coined by Peter Merholz, who jokingly broke the word weblog into the phrase "we blog." Blogging started as a way for individuals to share their thoughts, ideas, and daily experiences. Early examples were similar to digital diaries with text and images. It rapidly grew in popularity and evolved into a powerful medium for communication, influencing journalism, politics, and marketing.

Origins

Blogs have significantly impacted the way information is shared and consumed online. They allowed anyone with internet access to publish their thoughts and reach a global audience. Over time, blogs transformed into tools for businesses to connect with customers and build their brand.
